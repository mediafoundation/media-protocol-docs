**MEDIA** is the governance and utility token of the Media Protocol & Media Network. In its utility role, MEDIA serves as a medium of exchange between clients and providers. For governance, the decentralized nature of the protocol, which prevents control by a single entity, places oversight in the collective hands of individuals and entities. This democratic structure allows MEDIA token holders to cast votes on decisions important to the protocol.

### Exploring the Functions of MEDIA Token

The MEDIA token plays four key roles: as a commitment from registered providers, as a payment method for transactions, as a means for MEDIA holders to participate in the protocol's governance, and for treasury management.

### Compromiso

Los proveedores que quieran registrarse en marketplaces inicializados en el Media Protocol, deberan stakear un [Uniswap V3 Positions NFT](https://support.uniswap.org/hc/en-us/articles/20980786685069-Why-is-liquidity-position-ownership-represented-by-tokens-or-NFTs-) que represente un valor minimo de acuerdo a los parametros establecidos por el dueño del marketplace en cuestión. Para obtener estos NFT, los proveedores deben aportar liquidez en el par MEDIA/ETH en Uniswap V3.

Al stakear NFT con la posición con el valor requerido, y, estando éste [en rango](https://support.uniswap.org/hc/en-us/articles/20980601560717-What-is-a-range-order-), los proveedores podrán entonces ofrecer servicios y interactuar con clientes. Este mecanísmo es escencial para que los proveedores demuestren su compromiso con el marketplace y contribuyan a la confianza y estabilidad del protocolo.

### Transactions

Providers price their offers in MEDIA tokens. Clients use MEDIA tokens to secure these offers, leading to formalized deals. Billing is based on the duration and rate agreed upon in these deals. At the conclusion or cancellation of a deal, tokens are distributed to the provider for services rendered, with any unused tokens returned to the client. This ensures fairness and efficiency in transactions.

### Governance

MEDIA token holders guide the Media Protocol's direction. Through a DAO, they can vote on key decisions, influencing the protocol's evolution and policies.
For more information, please refer to the [governance docs](/governance/overview).

### Treasury Management

Transaction fees generated within the protocol contribute to a treasury. MEDIA token holders have a say in how these funds are allocated, used, or distributed. Essentially, the MEDIA token allows holders to manage and direct the protocol's financial resources.
